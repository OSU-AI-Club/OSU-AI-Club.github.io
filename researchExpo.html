The OSU AI Research Expo

Registration Form: Link

Event Description: The Research Expo is a one day event where faculty / Grad Students have the opportunity to present their findings to other research groups and students. This event helps two groups in two different ways. It helps students by allowing them to understand new updates in this growing field. It is helps faculty members by potentially having a student working within their lab and also helps them recieve crucial feedback on their research.
Eligibility: Open to all students with any background interested to learn more about AI and its various applications

FAQ:
I've never attended an Artificial Intelligence research expo, can I still attend?
Yes! Everyone is welcome to attend!

What if I don't have anything to present?
That's completely ok. We will have faculty members ready to present so that students don't need to worry about that. If they would like to present, please email the OSU AI Club (found below)

Who hosts Research Expo?
Research Expo is hosted by a group of students from the Artificial Intelligence Club at Ohio State.

Do I need to be a part of the AI Club to attend in HackAI?
No! All students are welcome to attend!

I attend another university, can I still attend
Yes! All students attending a US university are allowed to attend.
