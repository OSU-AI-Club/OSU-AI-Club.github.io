Who We Are

We are a student organization run by passionate students and researchers.

Our goal is to educate, inspire, and connect students on the topic of Artificial Intelligence.

We're proud to have hosted Intel, Microsoft, and many others.

What We Do

We bring in speakers from the industry and academia.

We host our own workshops for students to learn about AI.

Once a year, we put on an Artifical Intelligence hackathon called HackAI.

Get Involved

Want to join?

Everyone of all skill levels is welcome! If you are interested in joining make sure to subscribe to our newsletter, join the Discord, fill out the registration form, and come to our weekly meetings every Monday 7:00-8:00 pm in Hitchcock 035!

Want to Present?

Many AI topics are wanted. When you're ready, email us, and we'll discuss what events are possible.
