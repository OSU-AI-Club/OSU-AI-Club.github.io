Who are we?

We are a student organization run by passionate students and researchers. Our goal is to educate, inspire, and connect students on the topic of Artifical Intelligence. We've proud to have hosted Intel, Microsoft, and many others.

What do we do?

We bring in speakers from the industry and academia. In addition, we host our own workshops for students to learn about AI. Once a year we put on an artifical intelligence focused hackathon called HackAI.

Want to join?

Everyone of all skill levels is welcome! Join the mailing list to learn about upcoming meetings and opportunities.

Want to present?

Many AI topics are wanted. When you're ready, email us, and we'll discuss what events are possible.

Want to sponsor?

Sponsors get many benefits, including access to resumes. Please email us for further information.
